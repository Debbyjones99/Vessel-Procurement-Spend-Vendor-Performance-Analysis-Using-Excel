# Vessel-Procurement-Spend-Vendor-Performance-Analysis-Using-Excel

##Project Overview

This project analyzes procurement activities for vessel operations using a real-world company procurement dataset. The analysis was carried out entirely in Microsoft Excel to understand spending patterns, vendor performance, procurement trends, and recurring operational purchases.

The project focuses on identifying procurement inefficiencies, monitoring operational spending, and uncovering opportunities for cost reduction through better purchasing strategies and vendor management.

##Business Objectives

* Analyze month-on-month procurement spend
* Evaluate vendor performance and procurement concentration
* Assess procured items
* Identify drivers of high procurement spending
* Detect frequently requested or excess procurement items
* Monitor overall spend trends over time
* Recommend cost optimization opportunities
